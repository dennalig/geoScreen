Brief Walkthrough

Here is the Interface. It displays the detected location based on your Ip Address.

If the location that is detected is wrong or not what you like, you have the option to enter in your specific city, state (if applicable), and country. Click set location to recieve a new set of images based on your new entered location. Alternativley, clicking refresh location will default the location to the one detected by your IP address.

DISCLAIMER: Selecting 'Set Location' or 'Reset Location' will replace ALL current image files in the 'Geo Images' folder.

The Save Folder of Geo-Screen is 'Geo Images'. This folder will store images and be updated accordingly. Go to the path specified by 'SAVE FOLDER' to view or alter the contents.
